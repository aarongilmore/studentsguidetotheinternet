Student's Guide to the Internet

A collection of resources for students of design.

About

This website serves as a repository of information for students of design; the best books, articles, and media relating to design and technology that I've come accross in the past few years.

It is, of course, not limited to students and my hope is anyone can find something useful here. This is only a taste of the incredible wealth of knowledge available to us.
